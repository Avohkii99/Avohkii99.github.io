Manning's Equation:

v = (1/n) * R^(2/3) * S^(1/2)

where:

  • v: Flow velocity (m/s or ft/s)
  • n: Manning's roughness coefficient (dimensionless)
  • R: Hydraulic radius (m or ft)
  • S: Channel slope (m/m or ft/ft)
